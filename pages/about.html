About Us

Enriching education in Council Rock schools since 2002 through innovation, community support, and inspired learning.

Our Mission

The Council Rock Education Foundation (CREF) is an independent, non-profit organization dedicated to funding innovative educational experiences that enrich Council Rock schools and inspire lifelong learning in every student.

We partner with educators, parents, and the community to provide resources beyond the standard district budget, fostering creativity, curiosity, and excellence.

Our History

Founded in 2002 by a group of passionate parents and community members, CREF has grown into a vital force for educational enhancement in the Council Rock School District.

Over the past two decades, we have awarded more than 150 grants, impacting thousands of students with hands-on programs, advanced technology, creative arts initiatives, and much more.

Our continued success is thanks to generous donors, dedicated volunteers, and strong partnerships with the school district.
